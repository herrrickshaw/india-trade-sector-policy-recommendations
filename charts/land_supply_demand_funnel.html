Land required vs land available — the readiness funnel

Pending industrial-investment intent (IEM) converted to acres, against the supply of industrial land narrowing by readiness. The point: India advertises millions of acres, but only ~261,570 are developed-and-vacant nationally.

Sources: pending IEM demand (DPIIT Part A minus Part B, Rs 14.3 lakh cr) converted to acres via land-intensity benchmarks (bulk-drug parks ~1 Rs cr/acre to medical-device parks ~16); supply from India Industrial Land Bank (developed-park vacant, 253,720 ac national), state land banks (Odisha IDCO 104,026 ac undeveloped), and distressed pools (GIDC resumed + PSU/NLMC). Data: scenarios, advertised land. The three demand bars ARE the uncertainty (land-intensity is assumed, not known); ready-land supply is the clean national figure.
